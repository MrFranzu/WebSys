Anime


I have liked anime shows ever since I was in the 4th grade. Unlike cartoons, anime hits me differently, those nostalgic shows like Ghost Fighter and Slam Dunk. I remember watching it on Tagalog dub on GMA and I was amazed how the shows progressed and it’s full of action packed scenes. When I hit 5th grade, we had a new laptop and internet connection, that's when I started watching anime online every day. Until now, I still watch anime, but unlike before I spend like 10 hours a day watching, I now have responsibilities at home and for studies. Although, I still watch everyday but for only like 3 hours at night time.
